Brittany Cortez

Brittany Cortez is a Journalism and Design freshman at The New School, with plans to graduate May of 2021. Throughout her time in the program, she has covered the multifaceted neighborhood of Bushwick, ranging from its stark wealth disparity to its makeshift arts and culture. In tandem with her journalism class, she takes Intro To Nonfiction, where she has produced an essay about her ethnic identity. In the future, Brittany plans to work for an NYC publciation, and to one day publish a book of personal essays.
